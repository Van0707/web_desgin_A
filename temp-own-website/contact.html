Directions

How to Find Us

From Rochester: Take Route 19 South for about 45 minutes. We're located right in downtown Warsaw at the intersection with Route 20A.

From Buffalo: Take Route 20A East through Arcade and continue to Warsaw. We're at the intersection of Route 20A and Route 19.

From Geneseo: Take Route 20A West directly to Warsaw. You'll find us at the crossroads with Route 19.

Parking: Street parking is available on Buffalo Street and nearby side streets. There's also a public parking lot just a block away.

Nearby Landmarks:

  • Warsaw Village Hall
  • Warsaw Central School
  • Oatka Creek Park

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer Wi-Fi?

Yes! We provide free Wi-Fi for all customers. Perfect for working, studying, or staying connected.

Can I bring my laptop?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to use our space for work or study. We have comfortable seating and power outlets available.

Do you cater events?

We can provide coffee and light refreshments for small events. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Is the Gathering Room always available?

The room is available when not booked for private events. We recommend calling ahead or making a reservation to ensure availability.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available for purchase in-store and make great gifts for coffee lovers.

Can I buy your coffee beans to take home?

Absolutely! We sell our premium Brazilian Santos coffee beans by the bag. Available in all our roast varieties.
