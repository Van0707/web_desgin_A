About Vertical Cafe

Learn more about our story, values, and commitment to the community

Our Story

At Vertical we desire for our customers to have a great experience when walking through the door. From the atmosphere to the drink or food we hope you leave never forgetting about your experience.

Vertical Cafe is a family owned and operated coffee shop located in downtown Warsaw, New York at the crossroads of Route 20A and Route 19. We strive to make it welcoming, comfortable, affordable and clean.

From our special lattes to our special paninis we know you will not leave disappointed. At Vertical people come from all around to rest, relax and revive with good coffee, good company, and great food!

Our Values

Rest

We provide a peaceful environment where you can take a break from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Our comfortable seating and calming atmosphere make it the perfect place to unwind.

Relax

Whether you're meeting friends, working on your laptop, or simply enjoying a quiet moment with your coffee, we want you to feel completely at ease in our space.

Revive

Our premium coffee and delicious food will energize you for the day ahead. We believe in the power of good coffee to revive both body and spirit.

Quality Coffee

We offer some of the best locally roasted coffee using premium "Brazilian Santos" beans. Our commitment to quality means we carefully select our beans and roast them to perfection.

Our Roasts Include:

  • Dark Roast - Rich and bold
  • Blonde Roast - Light and smooth
  • Jamaican Roast - Exotic tropical flavor
  • Italian Roast - European style
  • Decaf - All the flavor, none of the caffeine

Each cup is crafted with care by our experienced baristas who are passionate about delivering the perfect coffee experience.

What We Offer

Internet Cafe

Stay connected with our free Wi-Fi and comfortable workspaces. Whether you're working remotely, studying, or just browsing, we provide a productive environment for all your digital needs.

Conference Room

The Vertical "Gathering Room" is perfect for business meetings, private parties, study groups, or any gathering. Equipped with modern amenities and a professional atmosphere.

Gift Shop

Take a piece of Vertical home with you! Our gift shop features branded merchandise including t-shirts, caps, mugs, and of course, our premium coffee beans.

Part of the Community

As a family-owned business, we're proud to be part of the Warsaw community. We believe in supporting local suppliers, creating jobs, and providing a gathering place where neighbors can connect.

Located at the intersection of Route 20A and Route 19, we're easily accessible and serve as a hub for both locals and travelers passing through our beautiful area.
